Hello, how can I help you?

An affordable internet based customer service solution.

Let me introduce myself

SonicChat offers your business the unique opportunity of assisted customer service and management. In other terms, our team of customer service representatives handle your customers inquires via our intuitive chat widget located on your company website. A chat widget encourages customers to ask questions and seek help without the hassle of a prompted telephone service and any additional wait time. A chat widget alone is designed and implemented to increase customer experience and satisfaction which increases company sales. However, this is not your ordinary chat widget. SonicChat stores all chat records, schedule appointments, open tickets and carry out other administrative tasks, much like a personal secretary.

How is all of this possible?

Our powerful dashboard equips our customer service representatives with access to our company Answer-Base system. The SonicChat Answer-Base houses supplied company and product documentation. In other terms, you supply us with popular or possible questions and answers and we take it from there. You can add new Q&A to the answer-base through your admin dashboard as often as you would like. SonicChat is designed to grow and change with evolving businesses.

Why use SonicChat?

SonicChat supplies your company with its own independent full time customer support center and personal secretary. This solution encourages an increase in customer relations, satisfaction and support all while saving your company thousands of dollars in employment cost and training. In addition, by taking advantage of our cutting-edge appointment and ticket system(s), we guarantee a smooth interactive experience for you and your customers.
