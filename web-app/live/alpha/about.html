Learn More About Me

An affordable internet based customer service solution.

How can I help you?

SonicChat offers your business the opportunity of assisted customer service and management. In other words, our team of customer service representatives handle your customer inquires via our intuitive chat widget located on your company website. A chat widget encourages customers to ask questions and seek help, which increases satisfaction and leads to a point of sale. However, this is not your normal chat widget. SC can keep records, make appointments, open tickets and carry out similar tasks, much like a personal secretary.

How is all of this possible?

Our powerful dashboard equips our customer service representatives with access to our company Answer-Base system. The SonicChat Answer-Base houses supplied company and product documentation. You supply us with possible questions and answers and we take it from there. You can add new Q&A to the answer-base through your admin dashboard as much as you like. SonicChat is designed to grow and change with evolving businesses.

Why use SonicChat?

SonicChat supplies your company with its own full time customer support center and personal secretary. This solution encourages increased customer relations, satisfaction and support while saving you thousands in employment cost and training. Also, by taking advantage of our appointment and ticket systems you can assure a smoother communication experience for you and your customers.
