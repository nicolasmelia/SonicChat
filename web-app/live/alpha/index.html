Proactive engagement

SonicChat offers the convenience of on-demand support and encourages customer interaction. Our team of customer service representatives handle your customer inquires via our intuitive chat widget located on your company website.

Personal Secretary

This is not your normal chat widget. Much like a personal secretary, SC can keep records, make appointments, open tickets and carry out similar tasks. By taking advantage of our appointment and ticket systems you can assure a smoother communication experience for you and your customers.

Increased Sales

This solution will grow customer relations while saving your company thousands in employment cost and training. A chat widget encourages customers to ask questions and seek help, which increases satisfaction and leads to a point of sale.

SonicChat takes advantage of two way (3DES) data encryption. This means your customer information and full chat history is safe with us.
